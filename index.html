About Me

Who Am I

I am Francisco Ochoa, a Full Stack Developer and a Website Designer based in South Central Los Angeles, California, USA. I aspire to create beautiful and stunning websites that combat the root causes of problems my clients face. I use Human-Centered Design thinking in every project to ensure quality, usability, and effortless design from a user perspective.

When I am not busy programming or designing, I enjoy riding my cool fixie down the Grande Ave. hill in Downtown Los Angeles. Maybe you can catch a flick of me bombing down traffic.
